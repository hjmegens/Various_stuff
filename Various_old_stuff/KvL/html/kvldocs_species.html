Species

Searches in FanSnIP can be done based on one of the following species:

  • chicken (Gallus gallus)

  • cattle (Bos taurus)

  • pig (Sus scrofa)

  • human (Homo sapiens)

  • house mouse (Mus musculus)

  • dog (Canis familiaris)

  • zebrafish (Danio rerio)

Chicken, cattle and pig are our primary focus, but any species can be included provided there is a genome (gene) and variation database in EnsEMBL. Human and mouse have been added primarily for developmental purposes and because these species have been most studied.

As currently there is no (main) EnsEMBL database entry for pig, there is only very limited functionality for this species. When that database is available in the future, it can be added with little effort.

Genes and genenames that get retrieved during the GeneSearch will be species specific. Depending on the options for GeneSearch, the gene names will either be retrieved directly from the species-specific database or the orthologues of human or mouse genes will be returned.
